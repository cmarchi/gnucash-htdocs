Net Worth Barchart

Table of Contents

Your net worth is the difference between the value of your assets or liabilities - a measure of your wealth, in other words. As such, it's interesting and informative to track this information over time. This bar chart lets you view how it (hopefully) grows

Options

  • Accounts

  • Report Name

  • Stylesheet

  • From, To

  • Step Size

  • Report Currency

  • Plot Width, Height

  • Show Asset & Liability Bars

    Show a pair of bars for each time step representing your assets and liabilities, respectively

  • Show Net Worth Bars

    Show a a bar in each time step representing your overall net worth at that time.
