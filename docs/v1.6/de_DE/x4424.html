Docking, Moving, and Tearing Off

If you have the right GNOME settings, you can move the menu bar, toolbar, and summary bar around as you please by simply clicking on the handle on their left, and dragging them where you want. You can even drag them completely outside the GnuCash window, if you so choose. If you can't do this, you may need to change in the "Look and Feel" tab of the GNOME configuration tool.
