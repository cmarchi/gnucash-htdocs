The Toolbar and the Summary Bar

The toolbar is located immediately below the menu bar, and provides shortcuts to commonly used functions. As with the menu, it changes depending on whether the window contains a report or an account tree.

The Summary Bar displays your net assets and profit/earnings at a glance.
