Asset and Liability Barcharts

Table of Contents

These reports allow you to display home one, some, or all of your assets or liabilities change over time. If you want a barchart showing an overall picture of your net worth, use the Net Worth Barchart instead.

Options available for this report include:

  • Accounts

  • Report Title

  • From & To

  • Step Size

  • Style Sheet

  • Price Source

  • Depth

  • Add More!!!
