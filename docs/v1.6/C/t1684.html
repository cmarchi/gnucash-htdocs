Balance Sheet

Table of Contents

This report summarizes your assets, liabilities, and equity. When your books are properly maintained, your assets should equal the sum of your liabilities and equity. If that is not the case, there is some kind of internal imbalance in your accounts.

Options available for this report include:

  • Accounts

  • Depth

  • Report Title

  • Date

  • Style Sheet

  • Price Source

  • Add More!!!

Note that while you can select specific accounts for this report, unless you know exactly what you are doing it is unwise and highly likely to give you misleading and incorrect figures.
