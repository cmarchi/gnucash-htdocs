10.4. Setting International Preferences

GnuCash allows you to specify the following international related preferences

  • Default Currency for accounts in the Accounts tab under Edit->Preferences

  • Default Report Currency in the Reports tab under Edit->Preferences

  • Time Format and Date Format in the Date/Time tab under Edit->Preferences

For a full explanation of the International preferences available, see the GnuCash Help Manual.
