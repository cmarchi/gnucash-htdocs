2.3. GnuCash On-line Assistance

Quoting the Tip of the Day "The GnuCash developers are easy to contact." GnuCash has several mailing lists;

  • User List <gnucash-user@gnucash.org> - User questions and discussions. The developers and many very helpful users "hang-out" here.

  • Development List <gnucash-devel@gnucash.org> - Discussion of issues related to the design and development of GnuCash and submission of patches.

  • You can chat live with the developers on IRC! Join them on #gnucash at irc.gnome.org. Much more at the GnuCash web site including links to WIKI and Bugzilla !!
